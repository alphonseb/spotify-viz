Did the Woodstock festival shake up the music industry ?

On August 15, 1969, the mythical Woodstock festival took place where nearly half a million people attended over three days. Folk, rock and blues artists such as Santana and Jimi Hendrix performed there. This festival is considered one of the greatest moments in the history of popular music.

At the end of the 1960s, the hippie movement was at its peak. Pacifists and intellectuals gathered in huge demonstrations in favor of peace. the protests are rising sharply and a part of the youth enters in revolt.

We know that this period has greatly contributed to the release of some morals and this counter culture embodies the development of new social movements.
